League Rules


* Picks must be made before Thursday night’s kickoff. However, you can still modify your picks until the kickoff of that game or Sunday at 1 p.m. After that, all picks for that week are final.

* Please use the “Best Bet” box exclusively for the night games (Thursday Night, Sunday Night, and Monday Night).

* Members earn 3 points for correctly selecting any of the Sunday “Day” games.

* Members earn 7 points for correctly selecting any of the “Night” games (Thursday, Sunday, Monday) by selecting the “Best Bet” box to the right of the games when making your weekly picks.

* Any of the “Best Bet” games that are not correctly selected will be modified by the Commissioner.

* One member will be awarded 10 bonus points each week for having the best record for that week.
